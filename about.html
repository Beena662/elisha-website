About Elisha

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Elisha Fashion Studio blends South‑Asian textile heritage with modern silhouettes. From university‑ready kurtas to hand‑embroidered luxury ensembles, every piece is crafted with uncompromising attention to detail and a commitment to ethically sourced fabrics.

Craftsmanship & Values

We collaborate with 350+ artisans across Pakistan, empowering local communities while ensuring fair wages and safe workplaces. Our design philosophy is simple — timeless elegance, premium fabric, and a fit that flatters every woman.
